Cavetto

shelving system | 2016

This shelving system, made of solid wood, is the perfect hybrid between german clarity and scandinavian warmth with nice crafted details. The ingenious thing about Cavetto is that the edges of the shelves are shaped so that back and side panels can be inserted between them without the need for any fittings.
