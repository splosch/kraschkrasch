Rail

table system | 2016

With its connectable modular components, RAIL offers a range of tables that can be combined to create structured spaces in offices, studios or agencies. Jointless table tops and movable trestles define the system’s ‚studio‘ character. Quickly and easily extendable, RAIL allows for flexible interior concepts. RAIL’s light and studio-like appearance can also be transferred to the home.
