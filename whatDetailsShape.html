Shape

table | 2015

Shape is an elegant and filigree table combined with a solid stability. The frame of the table is made of laser cutted steel. After the cutting process the y-shaped sides are folded and thereby reach high stability. The crossbars connect the sides. This table fits into many places like dining and living areas or offices and conference rooms.
